Erase Trash

By default, starting Permanent Eraser will erase the files contained in your Trash. The program will give a warning before it begins erasing the files.

With macOS Catalina and later, Full Disk Access needs to be enabled to allow Permanent Eraser to erase the contents of the Trash.
