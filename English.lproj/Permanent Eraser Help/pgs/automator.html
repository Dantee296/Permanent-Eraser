Automator Actions

Permanent Eraser comes with the following Automator Actions to aid in automating your tasks to erase files.

  • Erase Free Space: Erase the free space on a selected disk.
  • Erase Selected Items: Erase the selected files.
  • Erase Trash: Erase the files in the Trash.
